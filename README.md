# NoteSprint

Welcome to **NoteSprint**! 

NoteSprint is a beautiful, minimal, and mobile-friendly study app. It helps you quickly practice and review concepts using flashcards and a revision mode.

### Features
- **Flashcard Mode**: Test your knowledge actively. Read the question, guess the answer, and tap to reveal it.
- **Revision Mode**: Quickly scan through questions and answers together for rapid review.
- **Customizable Sessions**: Choose exactly how many basic, medium, and hard questions you want to practice.
- **Built-in Timer**: Set a time limit for your sessions to keep yourself focused.
- **Beautiful Design**: A modern, clean interface that works perfectly on both your phone and your computer.

### How to use
1. Browse through the available topics on the Dashboard.
2. Select a topic and configure your session (choose difficulty levels and time).
3. Start learning!

Happy studying! 🚀
